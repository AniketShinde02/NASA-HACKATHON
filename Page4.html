Our Goals

To determine the abundance and distrubution of extra solar planets as a function of size,orbital radius,and other factors in our galaxy

To understand the characteristic of exoplanet and exoplanetary system including the formation, evolution and composition.

To find planets that maybe habitable and search for the fingerprints of extant life on those planets.
